# fnnlr — Investor / Partner Proof

Honest, evidence-backed. No guaranteed-revenue claims, no fabricated TAM, no
fabricated traction. Where a market number would go, we say it must be sourced
externally rather than invented.

## Category thesis
In Egypt and the Gulf, the real commerce motion is a WhatsApp conversation plus a
manual/local transfer — not card checkout and automated drip. Tools built for the
Western funnel assume the wrong primitives. fnnlr is built for the actual motion.

## Market wedge
Arabic-native, WhatsApp-first, manual-payment-aware revenue operations with an
evidence-based Revenue Desk — a combination the incumbents do not center.

## Why Arabic-native matters
The product, copy, and operating guidance are Egyptian-Arabic-first, not a
translation layer — closer to how the merchant actually works.

## Why WhatsApp-first matters
The sales spine is WhatsApp; the 24h service window and inbound signal are
first-class, not bolted on.

## Why manual-payment awareness matters
Local manual payment (InstaPay, Vodafone Cash, bank transfer, Fawry, cash) is part
of the funnel as recorded **state** — the funnel reflects reality instead of
assuming card checkout.

## Product depth
35 modules across funnel building, capture, payments, Revenue Desk, opportunities,
attribution, recommendations, playbooks, portfolio, activation, execution,
operating room, deployment, and repeatability — 52 test files, 29 tenant
migrations, a real-Postgres live suite.

## Moat (built, not promised)
- Evidence memory — record before advising.
- Outcome learning — rate over decided outcomes only.
- Attribution learning — confidence gated by sample size.
- Recommendation learning — every mutating action approved.
- Playbooks — repeatable, evidence-driven plays.

## Safety as a feature
Human approval for mutating actions; no fake revenue; no auto-send; no payment
processing; fail-closed encryption; no `x-tenant-id` trust.

## Technical credibility
Database-per-tenant isolation, a real-Postgres live test suite, a one-command CI
that says SAFE / NOT SAFE TO RELEASE, and a proven customer repeatability path.

## Commercial readiness
Honest commercial packaging, sales qualification + fit scoring, a deployment pack,
and a sales/support operating system — all guarded by a consistency checker that
fails the build on any forbidden claim.

## Honest remaining risks
- No real customer traction is claimed; there is no customer data in this pack and
  none is fabricated. Any market-size figure must be sourced externally.
- Production deployment guards are proven; a managed-Postgres backup/restore drill

- Integrations, ecosystem, billing, and enterprise admin are intentionally
