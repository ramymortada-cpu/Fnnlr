# fnnlr — Commercial FAQ

**Does fnnlr auto-send WhatsApp messages?**
No. fnnlr does not auto-send. It prepares drafts and tracked links; a human on
your side sends the messages.

**Does it process payments?**
No. fnnlr does not process payments and does not move money. It records manual
payment state (InstaPay, Vodafone Cash, bank transfer, Fawry, cash). You collect
and confirm payment.

**Does it guarantee sales?**
No. There are no guaranteed results — no guaranteed revenue, sales, or ROI.
Outcomes depend on your offer, traffic, and how you respond to leads. fnnlr shows
you, with evidence, what to do next.

**Does it work without traffic?**
It runs, but it has little to measure. fnnlr is evidence-based: with no traffic,
there are no signals, so recommendations are limited and say so. You need a
traffic source.

**Do I need a team to respond?**
You need at least one person to respond to leads and one to confirm payments
(can be the same person). fnnlr does not reply for you.

**Does it support Arabic?**
Yes. fnnlr is Arabic-native (Egyptian Arabic with English technical terms),
built for the Egyptian and Gulf sales motion.

**Does it work in Egypt and the Gulf?**
Yes — that is the target market, with local manual payment methods as a
first-class part of the funnel.

**Do I need a WhatsApp BSP?**
Not to start. fnnlr is manual-link-first. A BSP webhook is optional for inbound
signal and is connected server-side; it is never required to launch.

**Is there AI?**
Yes — for funnel generation and for evidence-based recommendations and repairs.
When the AI key is unavailable, fnnlr falls back to a degraded mode that is marked
as such; it never pretends. All AI suggestions need your manual approval.

**What happens if there isn't enough data?**
fnnlr says so. It records before it advises. With thin data, the Revenue Desk
shows activation steps instead of fabricated opportunities, and confidence is
never reported as high. It does not invent numbers.
