## Report Requirements

Ask reporters to include:

- Affected endpoint or workflow.
- Reproduction steps.
- Impact description.
- Whether any tenant/customer data was accessed.
- Screenshots or logs with secrets redacted.

## Safe Harbor Position

No public safe-harbor statement is final until founder/legal approval.

## Internal Handling

1. Assign severity.
2. Create incident/security issue.
3. Confirm whether tenant isolation, auth, secrets, or PII are affected.
4. Patch or mitigate.
5. Record evidence and customer communication decision.
