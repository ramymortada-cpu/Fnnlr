# Industry Template: Ecommerce

## Goal

Recover high-intent shoppers and improve follow-up around manual or assisted checkout.

## Funnel

1. Product/offer page.
2. WhatsApp tracked CTA.
3. Product fit qualification.
4. Payment-state follow-up.
5. Delivery/support handoff.

## WhatsApp Sequence

- First reply: confirm product and quantity.
- Follow-up 1: answer delivery/payment questions.
- Follow-up 2: payment reminder if customer is waiting.
- Follow-up 3: delivery confirmation support.

## Qualification Rules

- Product selected.
- Quantity.
- Delivery city.
- Payment preference.
- Urgency.
