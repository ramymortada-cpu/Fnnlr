# fnnlr — Customer Proof Pack

For the customer/operator. Plain language, no internal stack traces, no overclaim.

## What you get
A hosted offer page, tracked WhatsApp links, recording of real signals (views,
clicks, leads, manual payment states), and an evidence-based Revenue Desk — plus
guided activation, go-live, and first-week operating support.

## What happens in the first 7 days
- Day 0: config validation + setup + activation.
- Day 1: publish + tracked link + first-signal check + execution lock.
- Day 2–3: 72h monitor + a safe update.
- Day 7: first-week review and a clear decision.
(Each step is a real command — see `ONBOARDING_PROMISE.md`.)

## What fnnlr watches
Activation progress, first page view, first WhatsApp click, first lead, manual
payment states, and the Revenue Desk — surfacing blockers, never hiding them.

## What we need from you
WhatsApp number, offer details, payment instructions, traffic source, a person to
respond to leads, and a person to confirm payments. (Customer responsibilities are
listed in the agreement.)

## What fnnlr will not do automatically
- It will **not** auto-send WhatsApp messages — you send them.
- It will **not** process payments — it records manual payment state; you confirm.
- It will **not** guarantee revenue or sales.
- Recommendations require your approval and appear only with enough observed data.

## How success is measured
Activation success (page live, link live, first event + lead, an actionable desk
item) and operational success (daily check runs, blockers visible, next action
clear). Revenue is reported only if real payment states exist — no fake ROI.

## What happens if blocked
A BLOCKED status is surfaced with the missing step and what to do next. Critical
issues carry an owner and a next action. We never tell you a BLOCKED state is fine.

## How support works
Operator-assisted within the agreed launch window, with named owners (setup,
support, response, payment confirmation, rollback) and a single safe support pack.

## Proof that setup is repeatable
fnnlr runs a second customer through the same path with full isolation — distinct
tenant, idempotent setup, and a smoke for one customer never touching another.
This is proven by an automated repeatability check on a real database, not
asserted.

## GTM proof readiness
Partner and case-study proof are evidence-gated. Partner distribution remains
`CONTRACT_READY_WITH_HOSTED_GAPS` until a real partner pilot is attached. Case
studies remain `HUMAN_ATTESTATION_REQUIRED` until customer approval, privacy
approval, and hosted metric evidence exist.
