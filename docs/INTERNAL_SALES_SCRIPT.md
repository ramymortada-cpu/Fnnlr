# fnnlr — Internal Sales Script

> For the operator running a sales call. Set honest expectations. Never promise
> auto-send, payment processing, or guaranteed revenue.

## 1. Discovery questions
- What do you sell, and what's the offer (promise, price, package)?
- How do customers reach you today — and where do most drop off?
- Do you sell over WhatsApp? Who replies to messages?
- How do you take payment? (Expect InstaPay / Vodafone Cash / bank / Fawry / cash.)
- Where does your traffic come from?
- Who confirms a payment came in?

## 2. Qualification (use SALES_QUALIFICATION.md)
Qualify in if there's a clear offer, a WhatsApp motion, a traffic source, manual
local payment, a responder, and willingness to do the 7-day setup. Flag anyone
who wrongly assumes auto-send or a sales guarantee for an expectation reset (the
product does neither).

## 3. Explain the product (one breath)
"fnnlr builds your offer page and tracked WhatsApp links, records what really
happens, and gives you a Revenue Desk of evidence-based next steps. It instruments
before it advises, and you approve every change."

## 4. Set expectations (say the limits, don't hide them)
- It does not auto-send WhatsApp — you send.
- It does not process payments — it records manual payment state; you confirm.
- It does not guarantee revenue — outcomes depend on your offer, traffic, and
  responses.
- Recommendations appear only when there's enough observed data.

## 5. Handle objections
- "Can it just message people for me?" → No, and that's deliberate — it keeps you
  compliant with WhatsApp and in control. It drafts; you send.
- "Can it take the payment?" → No — it records the payment state so your funnel is
  accurate; you collect and confirm.
- "Will it guarantee me X sales?" → No one honest can. It will show you, with
  evidence, where you're losing revenue and what to do next.
- "I don't have much traffic." → Then we start small and instrument; with no
  traffic there's little to measure, so a traffic source is part of the plan.

## 6. Close the next step
Propose the guided 7-day launch (Managed Launch). Agree the launch window and the
owners (responder, payment confirmer, rollback owner).

## 7. Collect before setup (the manifest inputs)
WhatsApp number · offer details · payment instructions · brand basics · traffic
source · support contact · response owner · payment confirmation owner · launch
window. (These map to `customer-zero.execution.example.json` and the
responsibilities checklist.)
