## What fnnlr will set up

  tooling.

## What the customer must provide
- WhatsApp number · offer details · payment instructions · traffic source ·
  brand basics (if needed) · a person to respond to leads · a person to confirm
  payments. (Full checklist in the agreement.)

## First 7-day plan
- Day 0: config validation + setup + activation.
- Day 1: publish + tracked link + first-signal check + execution lock.
- Day 2–3: 72h monitor + a safe customer update.
- Day 7: first-week review and a clear decision.

## What success means
- Activation success: page live, tracked link live, first event + first lead,
  an actionable Revenue Desk item.
- Operational success: daily check runs, blockers visible, next action clear.
- Revenue is reported only if real payment states exist — there is no fake ROI
  and no guaranteed revenue.

## What fnnlr will not do
- It will **not** auto-send WhatsApp messages — you send them.
- It will **not** process payments — it records manual payment state; you collect
  and confirm.
- It will **not** guarantee sales, revenue, or ROI.
- Recommendations require your manual approval and appear only with enough
  observed data.
