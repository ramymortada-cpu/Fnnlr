# Industry Template: Clinics

## Goal

Convert health/service inquiries into booked appointments without making medical claims.

## Funnel

1. Service page.
2. WhatsApp tracked CTA.
3. Appointment qualification.
4. Admin handoff.
5. Reminder and follow-up.

## WhatsApp Sequence

- First reply: ask service needed and preferred appointment time.
- Follow-up 1: clarify location and availability.
- Follow-up 2: reminder to confirm appointment.
- Follow-up 3: post-visit follow-up where appropriate.

## Qualification Rules

- Service requested.
- Preferred date/time.
- Branch/location.
- Contact details complete.

## Safety Boundary

No diagnosis, treatment promise, or medical advice should be generated.
