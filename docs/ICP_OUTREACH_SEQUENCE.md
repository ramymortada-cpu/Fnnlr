# ICP Outreach Sequence

## Target ICP

Arabic-first businesses where revenue workflows happen through WhatsApp and manual follow-up.

## Sequence

1. Problem opener: missed WhatsApp follow-up and invisible drop-offs.
2. Proof opener: show no auto-send/no payment-processing honesty.
3. Demo invite: 20-minute workflow visibility demo.
4. Pilot offer: assisted setup and first workflow launch.
5. Follow-up: share trust center and proof pack.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise revenue.
- Do not claim auto-send.
- Do not claim payment processing.
- Do not claim GA approval until GateForge evidence closes.
