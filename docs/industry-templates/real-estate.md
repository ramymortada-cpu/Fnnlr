# Industry Template: Real Estate

## Goal

Convert property inquiries into qualified WhatsApp conversations and scheduled visits.

## Funnel

1. Property/offer page.
2. WhatsApp tracked CTA.
3. Qualification questions.
4. Visit or call handoff.
5. Follow-up sequence.

## WhatsApp Sequence

- First reply: confirm area, budget, and timeline.
- Follow-up 1: share best-fit options.
- Follow-up 2: ask for preferred visit time.
- Follow-up 3: objection handling for price/location.

## Qualification Rules

- Budget range known.
- Area preference known.
- Buying/renting timeline known.
- Decision maker identified.

## Sales Handoff

Send lead summary with budget, area, property interest, and next action.
