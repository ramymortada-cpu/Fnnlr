## English

**Headline:** Sell on WhatsApp. See where your revenue leaks.

**Subheadline:** An Arabic-native, WhatsApp-first revenue operating system for
Egypt and the Gulf — built for manual, local payment.

**The problem:** Funnel tools assume card checkout and automated messaging. Your
real sales happen in a WhatsApp chat and a manual transfer. So you're stuck with
spreadsheets and guesswork about where leads drop off.

**How fnnlr works:** Build a hosted offer page and tracked WhatsApp links. fnnlr
records what really happens — views, clicks, leads, manual payment states — and
turns it into a Revenue Desk of evidence-based next steps. It instruments before
it advises, and you approve every change.

**What you get:** Hosted page · tracked WhatsApp links · manual-payment-aware
funnel · evidence-based Revenue Desk · activation, go-live, and first-week
operating support.

**What it does not do:** It does **not** auto-send WhatsApp messages. It does
**not** process payments. It does **not** guarantee revenue. You send the
messages, you confirm the payments, you approve the changes.

**Who it's for:** Small and growing businesses in Egypt and the Gulf that sell over
WhatsApp with local manual payment and have someone to respond to leads.

**Who it's not for:** Businesses wanting fully automated messaging, card payment
processing, or a guaranteed-sales machine.

**CTA:** Start a guided 7-day launch.

**Trust & safety:** Strict per-tenant isolation · encrypted, fail-closed
credentials · blockers always surfaced · no secrets in any output · no fabricated
numbers.
