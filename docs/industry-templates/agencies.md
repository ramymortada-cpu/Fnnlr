# Industry Template: Agencies

## Goal

Help agencies qualify leads and turn discovery conversations into scoped proposals.

## Funnel

1. Service/offer page.
2. WhatsApp tracked CTA.
3. Discovery qualification.
4. Proposal handoff.
5. Follow-up and objection handling.

## WhatsApp Sequence

- First reply: ask business type and goal.
- Follow-up 1: collect budget/timeline.
- Follow-up 2: suggest discovery call.
- Follow-up 3: proposal reminder.

## Qualification Rules

- Business type.
- Goal.
- Budget range.
- Timeline.
- Decision maker.

## Readiness Gate

Template doc is ready for controlled use. Promotion or repeatability claims require hosted template usage evidence from observed cohort outcomes.
