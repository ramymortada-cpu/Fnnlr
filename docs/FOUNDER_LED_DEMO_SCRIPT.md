# Founder-Led Demo Script

## Opening

fnnlr is an Arabic-first AI Revenue Operations OS for businesses that sell through WhatsApp, manual payments, and human-approved follow-up.

## Demo Flow

1. Show the offer/funnel page.
2. Show tracked WhatsApp CTA.
3. Show lead or signal capture.
4. Show Revenue Desk.
5. Show an evidence-based recommendation.
6. Show human approval boundary.
7. Show activation/operating-room status.
8. Show trust boundaries: no auto-send, no payment processing, no guaranteed revenue.

## Close

The paid pilot is about proving repeatable activation and workflow visibility, not promising revenue.
